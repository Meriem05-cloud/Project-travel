Djebel Bou Argoub

A beautiful mountain located in the Nabeul Governorate of Tunisia, at the entrance of the Cap Bon peninsula. Djebel Bou Argoub offers stunning panoramic views, rich Mediterranean vegetation, and peaceful hiking trails away from crowded tourist spots.

View on Google Maps View Trails
Hiking & Trekking Photography Nature & Peace
3–5 hours Easy to Medium Panoramic views
Advice: Visit in spring for wildflowers and mild weather.
Local advice: Start early morning to avoid heat. Bring water, snacks, and a camera for breathtaking views of the Cap Bon peninsula.

Dar Selma

A charming traditional guest house located in Bou Argoub, Nabeul Governorate. Dar Selma is known for its authentic Tunisian hospitality, peaceful atmosphere, panoramic views, and delicious homemade traditional cuisine.
