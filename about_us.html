About Explore Tunisia

Your gateway to Tunisia’s hidden wonders & timeless beauty

Our Mission

Explore Tunisia is born from a deep love for our country's rich tapestry — from the Mediterranean shores to the Sahara dunes, from Roman ruins to vibrant souks. We handpick authentic experiences, hidden gems, and cultural treasures to help you travel like a local. Whether you seek adventure, history, or relaxation, we're here to guide you.

Meet the creators

Two passionate explorers sharing Tunisia's soul with the world

Meriem Bouzid

Co-founder & Content Curator

Meriem researched and wrote the detailed guides for Bizerte, Nabeul, and Zaghouan. She focused on the pottery souks of Nabeul, the Roman water temples of Zaghouan, and the old port of Bizerte.Every recommendation comes from her own footsteps – from authentic 'Dars' to forgotten trails."

meriem.bouzid@polytechnicien.tn

Meria Hmema

Co-founder & Content Curator

Meria researched and wrote the detailed guides for Djerba, Kebili, and Mahdia. She covered the Chott el Djerid salt flats, Houmt Souk in Djerba, the Ottoman fortresses of Mahdia, and the desert oases of Kebili.
